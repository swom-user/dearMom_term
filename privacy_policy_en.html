Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Important: Dear Mom stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit any personal information to external servers.

1. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Dear Mom app collects the following information for the purpose of managing parenting activities and child development records:

  • Child's basic information (name, date of birth, gender, blood type)
  • Sleep records (sleep start/end times, notes)
  • Feeding records (feeding time, type, amount, notes)
  • Diaper change records (time, type, notes)
  • Parent mood/emotion records (date, emotional state, notes)
  • Diary entries (date, title, content, photos, tags)

2. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

All personal information is stored on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all data.

3. Storage Method of Personal Information

3.1 Local Storage

Dear Mom uses the Hive database to store all data locally on your device:

  • Data is not transmitted to external servers
  • Data is stored in the app's dedicated storage space on your device
  • Other apps or third parties cannot access your data

3.2 Photo Storage

Photos added in diary entries are:

  • Compressed and stored in the app's dedicated directory
  • Not transmitted or shared externally
  • Retained on your device until you delete them

4. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Dear Mom does not provide any personal information to third parties.

All data is stored only on your device, and no one, including the developers, can access your data.

5. Consignment of Personal Information Processing

Dear Mom does not outsource the processing of personal information to external parties.

6. Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects and How to Exercise Them

Users can exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Right to access: View all records within the app
  • Right to modify: Edit or update records
  • Right to delete: Delete individual records or all data
  • Right to suspend processing: Data processing stops immediately when you stop using the app

7. Procedure and Method of Personal Information Destruction

7.1 Destruction Procedure

  • Information deleted by users within the app is destroyed immediately
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data from your device

7.2 Destruction Method

  • Individual records: Use the delete function within the app
  • All data: Uninstall the app from device settings

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

When there are additions, deletions, or modifications to this privacy policy due to changes in laws, policies, or security technologies, we will notify users through app updates at least 7 days before implementation.

11. App Permissions

The Dear Mom app may request the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
Storage (Photos/Media/Files) Save and load diary photos Optional
Camera Take diary photos Optional

Note: Even if you deny optional permissions, the app's core functions (record management) will work normally, with only photo-related features being limited.

12. Measures to Ensure Security of Personal Information

Dear Mom protects personal information through the following technical and administrative measures:

  • Local storage: All data is stored only on your device with no network transmission
  • App sandboxing: Protected by the operating system's app isolation features
  • Access restrictions: Other apps or processes cannot access your data
  • Encrypted storage: Utilizes Hive database's built-in security features

13. Protection of Children's Personal Information

Dear Mom takes the following measures to protect children's (under 14 years old) information:

  • Children's information is collected with the consent of legal guardians (parents)
  • All data is stored only on the device and not transmitted externally
  • Legal guardians can view, modify, or delete children's information at any time

14. International Users

This privacy policy complies with Korean privacy laws. If you are using this app from outside Korea:

  • All data remains stored locally on your device regardless of your location
  • No data is transferred internationally as no external servers are used
  • You may be subject to local data protection laws in your jurisdiction

15. Data Portability

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • Data backup and transfer depend on your device's operating system features
  • The app does not provide cloud backup or synchronization services
